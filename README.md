# Global Consumer Electronics Supply Chain Intelligence Platform

## Overview

Global consumer electronics companies operate across highly interconnected manufacturing and supplier networks. This project builds a cloud-native analytics platform using Snowflake to analyze international trade flows, manufacturing trends, supplier concentration, and supply chain risks.

The platform uses official public trade datasets to help answer strategic business questions that product managers, supply chain analysts, and executives face when making sourcing and manufacturing decisions.

---

## Business Problem

How have global manufacturing hubs shifted over the last five years?

Can companies reduce supply chain risk by diversifying suppliers?

Which countries are becoming the next manufacturing leaders?

How concentrated is the electronics supply chain?
